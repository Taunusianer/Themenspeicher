Connect to Notion

This app saves directly to your Notion Tasks database.
You only need to do this setup once — your token is saved locally in your browser.

How to get your token:
1. Go to notion.so/profile/integrations
2. Create a new integration (name it e.g. "Tasks App")
3. Copy the Internal Integration Secret
4. In Notion, open the Tasks database → ··· menu → Connections → add your integration
